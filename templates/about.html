About SkillBridge

SkillBridge connects students with top companies for internships and gigs. Our mission is to empower the next generation of professionals with real-world experience.

Founded in 2023, we strive to make the search process seamless, transparent, and rewarding for both students and employers. Whether you're a student looking to kickstart your career or an employer seeking fresh talent, SkillBridge is your bridge to success.
